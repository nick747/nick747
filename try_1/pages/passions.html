# Passions

Leaving the world of programming, I have many passions. My favorite one is designing. I enjoy creating designs for apps and websites, like the one you are currently viewing, and finding ways to simplify processes. Whenever I use an app, I always consider how it could be improved by adding or removing features. I am particularly drawn to minimalism, which is evident in the design of this website.

Aside from designing, I have two other main passions which are Astronomy and Aviation. I am fascinated by the vastness and complexity of space. As I write this, a comet is going to be visible from Earth, and I am excited to witness it. In fact, I even created a recreation of Pandora from Avatar, complete with its gas giant:

Speaking of aviation, I have been fascinated by planes since childhood, which is reflected in my nickname, "Nick," while "747" comes from my favorite plane, the Boeing 747 (which unfortunately was announced to be retired last year). I enjoy playing aviation games like FlightGear (a free simulator) and Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020.
